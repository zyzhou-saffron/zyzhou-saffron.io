Packing Protocol

Posted on 2023-06-17 In 跳伞
Packing protocol in Strathallan Airfield

Check 1

  1. Confirm packing inspection.
  2. Canopy flaked.
  3. 9 packing tabs per side.
  4. Riser straight - NO TWIST.
  5. Stabiliser line check.
  6. Brake line check - NO TWIST.

    Sign for Check 1

Check 2

  1. Canopy folded correctly.
  2. Stabiliser out.
  3. No slack lines.
  4. Slider correctly in place up against stops. Lift slider to check brake lines are on top of stabilisers.

    Sign for Check 2

Check 3

  1. Check the condition of the bag and the elastic bungees.
  2. Check the condition of the static line, yellow cable pin and sliding snap hook. (open and close the hook)
  3. Ensure lines emerge from the centre of the bag.

    Sign for Check 3

Check 4

  1. Lines correctly stow.
  2. Lines clear of each other.
  3. Stows are securely held by elastic bungees.

    Sign for Check 4

Check 5

  1. Bag in the container correctly with lines to the top of the main tray.
  2. Risers correctly routed

    Sign for Check 5

Check 6

  1. Correct closing sequence bottom/top/left/right.
  2. Both cables through loop
  3. Routing of static line.
  4. S/L stowed correctly.
  5. Riser placed correctly.
  6. Final harness check.
  7. Ensure AAD is on.

    Sign for Check 6
    Attach packing ticket to equipment
